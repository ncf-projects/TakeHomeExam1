Our Covid Policy
In compliance with United States covid regulations, masks are strongly advised for all members attending an outdoor venue but are not forced. If the venue is indoors, usage of masks will be strongly inforced and regulated. Seats (if applicable) will be distanced three feet apart from each other to encourage social distancing. All atendees to any indoor or outdoor venue found to be violating our covid guidelines, they will be politely asked to leave the show, and will be issued no refund.
